# NovaTech Solutions: Internal Policies and Procedures

## 1. Working Hours and Operational Schedules
The standard operational schedule at NovaTech Solutions is structured around core collaboration hours. All full-time employees are expected to be accessible and actively participating from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM Eastern Standard Time (EST), regardless of geographic location. This ensures synchronous engineering updates and fluid cross-departmental coordination. A standard working week comprises forty hours, and flexible scheduling is permitted outside core collaboration windows, provided team deliverables are achieved on time and manager approval is documented.

## 2. Comprehensive Employee Benefits
NovaTech Solutions offers a robust competitive compensation and wellness framework:
* **Health and Wellness:** Full medical, dental, and vision insurance coverage starting from day one of employment, with premium costs fully covered by the organization for individual contributors.
* **Remote Work Stipend:** A one-time initial allocation of $2,500 to configure an ergonomic home workstation, supplemented by a recurring $150 monthly utility credit to offset high-speed internet costs.
* **Continuous Education:** An annual allocation of $5,000 for professional courses, technical bootcamps, academic certifications, and international engineering conference attendance.
* **Paid Time Off (PTO):** A baseline allowance of twenty-five business days of paid vacation per calendar year, plus ten international holiday options matching regional assignments.

## 3. Code of Conduct and Professional Ethics
NovaTech Solutions maintains an uncompromising stance on professional integrity, safety, and operational excellence. Employees must adhere to the following principles:
* **Inclusivity and Respect:** Zero tolerance for discrimination, harassment, or exclusionary actions based on race, gender, orientation, religion, or physical capability.
* **Data Confidentiality:** Intellectual property, client logs, source repositories, and configuration tokens are strictly confidential. Any export of proprietary material onto unencrypted personal devices constitutes a serious operational breach.
* **Open Source Stewardship:** Contributions made to external repositories on behalf of NovaTech Solutions must pass strict internal architectural sign-offs to guarantee compliance with existing software licenses.
